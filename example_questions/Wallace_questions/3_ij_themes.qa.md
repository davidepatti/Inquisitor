# 3 ij themes

## What is Ennet House in the novel?

- A recovery residence for people dealing with addiction
- A military base near Boston
- A private boarding school
- A TV production studio

## A major recurring theme in "Infinite Jest" is:

- Addiction and dependence
- Interplanetary travel
- Victorian courtship
- Corporate law litigation

## In the book's geopolitical setting, the U.S. is merged with Canada and Mexico under:

- O.N.A.N.
- N.A.T.O.
- U.N.E.S.C.O.
- E.U.

## The years in the novel are often identified by:

- Corporate sponsorship names
- Roman numerals only
- Names of U.S. presidents
- Meteorological seasons

## The tone of "Infinite Jest" is best described as:

- A mix of dark comedy and tragedy
- Purely romantic comedy
- Strict historical realism without satire
- A minimalist detective thriller

## The narrative structure of "Infinite Jest" is notably:

- Nonlinear and fragmented
- A strict day-by-day diary
- Entirely epistolary with letters only
- Written as a single courtroom transcript
